This Week In Science

Scientists have developed nanobots that precisely deliver clotting agents to areas of the body, which was done successfully in an animal study.

New analysis of mineralogy maps shows evidence of water across multiple locations on the surface of the Moon.

Extreme mental and physical fatigue experienced by many long COVID patients is observable in the central nervous system, research shows.

The US Food and Drug Administration approved the first nasal spray flu vaccine for self-administration.

A majority of coral reefs have been found to contain exceptionally heat-resistant coral, aiding the battle against the effects of climate change.

Researchers in Australia have found a whole new type of cell that helps grow blood vessels in instances of injury or poor blood flow.
